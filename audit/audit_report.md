## Executive Summary

### Current State
- **Overall Content Score**: 5.4/10 (below competitive threshold)
- **SEO Score**: 5.4/10 — missing geo-modifiers, weak keyword density, passive voice
- **GEO Score**: 5.3/10 — low AI citation potential, generic claims, no citable definitions
- **Social Score**: 5.5/10 — no hooks, too formal, missing trending vocabulary

### Target State (Post-Optimization)
- **Overall Content Score**: 8.8/10
- **SEO Score**: 9.0/10 — full keyword coverage, entity-rich, active voice
- **GEO Score**: 8.7/10 — 8 verbatim-quotable trigger phrases, citable definitions
- **Social Score**: 8.5/10 — 30 ready-to-use social hooks, platform-native language

### Key Findings
1. **"Platform pertama" claim buried in features** — should be in H1/hero
2. **Zero geo-modifier** ("Indonesia") in any heading — critical for local SEO/GEO
3. **No specific numbers** in hero section — competitors use 15+ data points
4. **Mixed language** (English headings on Indonesian page) — hurts NLP coherence
5. **Pricing/founding year incorrect** in llms.txt and schema — confuses AI crawlers
6. **No GEO trigger phrases** — content not structured for AI citation
7. **C-Level jargon** unclear to SMB audience — needs simplification

### Competitive Position
- **No GEO competitor in Indonesia** — GeoVera owns this category
- **No platform unifies SEO+GEO+Social** — unique differentiator
- **WhatsApp-first is unique globally** — every competitor uses web dashboards
- **Price gap**: Global GEO tools $495-$3000/mo vs GeoVera Rp 4.9-16.9jt/mo
